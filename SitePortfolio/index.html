Skills

Power BI

Business analytics tool that enables users to visualize and analyze data quickly and efficiently. It allows you to connect to various data sources, create interactive reports and dashboards, and share insights across your organization. With its user-friendly interface and robust features, Power BI helps businesses make data-driven decisions and gain valuable insights into their operations.

Power Apps

Empowers users to create apps tailored to their specific needs, integrating data from various sources such as Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365, and other third-party services. With its intuitive interface and drag-and-drop functionality, Power Apps enables rapid development and deployment of apps across web, mobile, and tablet devices.

Power Automate

Cloud-based service that allows users to automate workflows across multiple applications and services. It enables users to create automated processes to streamline repetitive tasks and integrate data between different systems. It helps organizations increase productivity, save time, and improve efficiency.

Dataverse

Secure and scalable data platform by Microsoft that allows organizations to store and manage business data used by Power Platform apps. It provides a centralized repository for storing structured data, enabling seamless integration with Power Apps, Power BI, and Power Automate.

Ai Builder

Easily incorporate AI capabilities into their business applications. Users can leverage capabilities such as form processing, object detection, prediction, sentiment analysis, and more to automate processes, gain insights, and enhance decision-making within their organizations. It integrates seamlessly with Power Apps and Power Automate, enabling users to add AI functionality to their apps and workflows with ease.
